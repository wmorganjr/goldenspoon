when you wish to suggest the current active top nav menu-item indicative of the page use the class hit. example below:

	<ul id="nav">
		
		<li class="hit" ><a href="/#menu_and_prices">Menu</a></li>			
		
		<li><a href="/generic.php">page</a></li>				
			
		<li><a href="/#location_and_hours">Location</a></li>
	
	</ul>
	
here, the menu link is active.
